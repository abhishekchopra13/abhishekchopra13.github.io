Here is a list of recommended books, podcasts, and online resources I find valuable:

### Recommended Books

*   **[Elements of Statistical Learning](https://hastie.su.domains/ElemStatLearn/)** by Trevor Hastie, Robert Tibshirani, and Jerome Friedman.
*   **[Advanced Futures Trading Strategies](https://www.systematicmoney.org/advanced-futures)** by Robert Carver.

### Podcasts & Video Channels

*   **[Flirting with Models](https://www.flirtingwithmodels.com/)** hosted by Corey Hoffstein.
*   **[Top Traders Live / Top Traders Unplugged](https://www.toptradersunplugged.com/)** hosted by Niels Kaastrup-Larsen.
*   **[Andrej Karpathy's YouTube Channel](https://www.youtube.com/@AndrejKarpathy)** – Invaluable, high-quality video tutorials and walk-throughs on building neural networks and understanding deep learning from scratch.

### Websites & Inspirations

*   **[LWN.net](https://lwn.net/)** – Executive news and coverage of Linux and open-source software.
*   **[Gregory Gundersen](https://gregorygundersen.com/)** – A beautifully designed personal webpage and blog that served as a major inspiration for setting up my own webpage.
